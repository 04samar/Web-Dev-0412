PERSONAL SKILLS

STEPS TO OPEN A DISCUSSION
  1. Be open-minded.
  2. Do not be dragged into an argument.
  3. Do not interrupt.
  4. Listen to the other person's point of view before giving your own.
  5. Do not exaggerate.
  6. Be enthusiastic in convincing, not forceful.
  7. Be willing to yield.
  8. Give your opponent a graceful way to withdraw without hurting his pride.
  9. Rejection can be hurtful.

During a discussion, it may be a good idea to use phrase such as

  • It appears to me.....
  • I may be wrong.....
IN ORDER TO BE A GOOD LISTENER
  1. Encourage the speaker to talk.
  2. Ask questions. It shows interest.
  3. Do not interrupt.
  4. Do not change the topic.
  5. Show understanding and respect.
  6. Pay attention, concentrate.
  7. Avoid distractions.
  8. Show empathy.
  9. Concentrate on the message and not on the delivery.
  10. Listen to feeling and not just words.
